About XCPFeeds.io
XCPFeeds.io is a Bitcoin oracle which broadcasts truth to the bitcoin blockchain. This site displays a list of future events which can be broadcast to the Bitcoin blockchain using the Counterparty platform.

What is Counterparty?
Counterparty is a new compliment to the Bitcoin protocol that performs general finance, user-defined asset exchange, and contract execution functions in a decentralized manner. To learn more about Counterparty you can visit counterparty.io or watch this quick video.
What is a Counterparty Broadcast?
A broadcast is the announcement of something to the counterparty network. There are a number of different uses for broadcasts. Chris DeRose does a good job of explaining what broadcasts are in this quick video.
What is a feed?

A feed is an event which has been broadcast on the counterparty network. A feed typically consists of 2 counterparty broadcasts.

The first broadcast is the announcement of the event information including details like the possible outcomes, the source of the information, and a deadline by which the result will be known.

The second broadcast is the announcement of the results of the event.

What are the fees for this service?

The current fee to broadcast an event is $1 per event.

The current fee for any user who uses the feed is 1%.

The collected fees are to cover our operating costs which include collecting and broadcasting the event and results information, bitcoin miners fees, server hosting, and to compensate our developers who maintain the site and its services.
